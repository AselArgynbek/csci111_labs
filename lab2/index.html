Intersting fact to know

Kyzylorda is the most Kazakh city in all of Kazakhstan: there is neither a noticeable number of Russians, as in the north of the country, nor Uzbeks, as in Turkestan or Shymkent; the share of the Kazakh population reaches 92%. Unlike the rest of the country, Russian is rarely used in everyday life: almost all signs are in Kazakh, and you will also be addressed in Kazakh if you yourself do not speak Russian. In addition, there is a noticeable Korean diaspora here - the second in the country after Almaty.
